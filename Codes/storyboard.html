Storyboard

Wireframe Layout

The wireframe design consists of a structured layout for each webpage, including:

  • Header: Site title and navigation menu.
  • Body: Varies by page with text, images, and interactive elements.
  • Footer: Displays copyright information.

List of Web Pages and Their Purpose

  • Home Page: Introduction to the site and overview of content.
  • Professional Page: Showcases academic and professional background, resume, and career goals.
  • Personality Page: Highlights personality traits, strengths, and values.
  • Personal Development Page: Discusses growth, learning experiences, and self-improvement journey.
  • Storyboard Page: Displays the website's planning and structure.
  • References Page: Lists sources, acknowledgments, and references.
  • Contact Page: Provides ways to reach out via email or social media links.

Theme of the Site

  • Color Scheme: Soft yellow tones for warmth and approachability, complemented by white and dark accents for readability.
  • Graphics: Clean and professional, with a balance between text and images.
  • Target Audience: Potential employers, academic professionals, and personal connections.

Website Features

  • Image Gallery: Showcasing professional work and hobbies.
  • Embedded Video: Introduction to myself and my career aspirations.
  • Interactive Navigation: Smooth transitions between pages.
  • External Links: Links to references, social media, and downloadable resume.
